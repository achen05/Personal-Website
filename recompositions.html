Aldon Chen

Recompositions

Completed in 2021 for MICA Prototype/Situate/Fabricate with Abigail Lucien.

Description

   Using clay made from the fibers of newspapers as the primary material, Recompositions aims to explore the current state of information, data, media, and their respective institutions. It is a fallen structure created in response to a growing shift in the re-decentralization of the internet and the methods in which information and data are distributed and organized. It hopes to capture the poetry of the moments following the collapse of a monument, ready to be decomposed, recyled, and then reconstructed once more.
