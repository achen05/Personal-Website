Dust Drawings

Visit Project

Process

  An interactive, generative drawing program that simulates Brownian Motion. Users create drawings that are always imperfect, incomplete, and impermanent.

  Dust Drawings was first conceived as an experimental, interactive background for my personal website that I ultimately decided to leave out. I’ve always noticed I was acutely aware of my relationship with my computers. It wouldn’t be too difficult to argue that I personified them even. It may come as no surprise then when I began to find the idea of creating art with my computer, as opposed to on it, compelling, poetic even. I figured that integrating an interactive simulation that reflected this relationship into my personal website would be an accurate way to communicate my interests. Users would experience literally ‘drawing’ with a computer.
