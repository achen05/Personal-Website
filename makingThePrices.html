Making the Prices

Completed in 2021 for MICA IA 277: Introduction to Robotic Arts

Description

   Stock market price data from the controversial GME short squeeze that occurred at the beginning of 2021, encoded in Code 39 barcode symbology and then printed on thermal paper using an Arduino-powered thermal printer. What new connections can be made when stock market data is printed onto a receipt? How do stock market prices resemble a grocery store? A postage confirmation? What does it mean to generate market data for machines?


Details

The most challenging part about this project was honestly trying to get the thermal printer module to even work. I originally planned to use the Circuit Playground Express but that was such a hassle with all the library installations that I ended up just buying an Arduino which was much easier to setup.
