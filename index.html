About

Currently unedited :)
 Hi there! I'm Aldon and I'm comfortable with any pronouns. I was born in San Francisco and raised all around the Bay Area which unsurprsingly led to my ongoing fascination with cutting edge technology. Combined with an early affinity for fine art and design, it seemed natural for me to explore the intersection between the three fields. I grew up jailbreaking and tweaking my iPhone, designing everything from bags to speculative technology to video games, and of course, a lot of drawing. Today, I'm particularly interested in designing and developing interfaces which includes not only those found in digital products, but also that of interactive art, experimental writing, physical products, and everything in between.
 I have special places in my heart for many things: low-tech, the Minecraft soundtrack, decentralized technology, and experimental electronic music among other things. When I'm not working on a project I'm most likely spending time with my family, thrift shopping with a friend, or listening to an audiobook by Maggie Nelson or Eileen Myles (yes, specifically those two authors) for seemingly the hundredth time while having a picnic at a nearby park.
