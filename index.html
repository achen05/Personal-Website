Aldon Chen

I’m Aldon, a New York-based designer at 2x4 with an interdisciplinary practice spanning visual identities, interfaces, and writing.

As a designer, my work serves a diverse range of sectors, including commerce, technology, public sectors, and culture. I have contributed to major projects for global clients such as Prada, Meta, and Hyundai, while maintaining an independent practice with organizations like Semiotext(e) and Luma Labs. My portfolio integrates typography and digital design with pursuits in fine art and critical writing.
